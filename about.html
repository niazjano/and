About Adrian & Sons Group Limited

We're a family-run construction company with years of experience in renovation, refurbishment, and home rebuilds. Our commitment is simple: quality workmanship, honest pricing, and customer satisfaction.

Our Story

Founded with a passion for quality construction and customer service, Adrian & Sons Group Limited has been transforming homes and exceeding expectations for years. We started as a small family business and have grown into a trusted name in local construction, but we've never lost sight of our core values: honesty, quality, and reliability.

Every project we undertake is treated with the same care and attention to detail, whether it's a simple kitchen upgrade or a complete home transformation. We believe that great construction isn't just about building—it's about creating spaces that families love to live in.

Our Process

1. Consultation

We discuss your project requirements, budget, and timeline. We'll visit your property to understand the scope of work.

2. Quote

You'll receive a detailed, fixed-price quote with no hidden costs. We explain exactly what's included and answer any questions.

3. Planning

We create a detailed project plan and timeline. We'll keep you informed throughout the process with regular updates.

4. Execution

Our skilled team works efficiently and professionally, maintaining high standards and keeping your property clean.

5. Completion

We ensure everything meets your expectations and leave your property spotless. We're always available for follow-up support.
